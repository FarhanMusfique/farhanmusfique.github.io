Debating Arena

This arena is for everyone who loves debating & wants to become a debater. Here I will write & share resources for them to learn debates step by step. Sometimes I will invite guest writers to write here. So it is going to be vast & a most helpful thing for any debater.

Why this? I also asked myself when this idea came to my mind. Debating is one of the lovely things people want to start with. I saw so many Kids with their debating enthusiasms but many times they just stopped debating & fell behind for their financial problems. Those kids cant manage a paid coach or resources that will help them to advance in this world. So I started this for them. I will provide the resources to develop themselves without them spending a single penny. Hope they will conquer the obstacles & achieve success.
