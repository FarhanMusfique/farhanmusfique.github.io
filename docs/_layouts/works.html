Practice Debates

Core Team Member

I'm a core team member of practice debates online, a platform found by a few high school and university students to do online debates for kids who live outside the megacity & can't partake in debates that much. Our goal is to provide them with a free online platform for practicing debates. We try to hold debates a few times a week and all you need to do to participate is send us a text and we'll add you to the messenger group. Our community also features regular free digital debate tournaments, frequent weekend practices and an assortment of training materials and live interactive workshops in an attempt to make debating as accessible as ever.
